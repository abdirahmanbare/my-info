Emergency Servicesv

Emergency Room (ER): Immediate treatment for acute illnesses and injuries.

Trauma Care: Specialized care for severe injuries, including surgeries.

Inpatient Servicesv

Medical/Surgical Units: Care for patients requiring hospitalization for various conditions.

Intensive Care Units (ICU): Specialized care for critically ill patients.

Maternity and Neonatal Units: Care for mothers and newborns.

Outpatient Services

Outpatient Clinics:Routine care, follow-up visits, and minor procedures.

Day Surgery: Procedures that dont require overnight stays.

Diagnostic Services

Imaging: X-rays, CT scans, MRI, ultrasound, and other imaging services.

Laboratory: Blood tests, urine tests, and other diagnostic lab work.

Pathology: Examination of tissues and samples for diagnosis.

Therapeutic Services

Physical Therapy: Rehabilitation services to restore function and mobility.

Occupational Therapy: Assistance with daily living activities and skills.

Speech Therapy: Treatment for speech, language, and swallowing disorders.

Surgical Services

General Surgery: Broad range of surgical procedures.

Specialized Surgery: Orthopedic, cardiovascular, neurological, etc.

Pharmacy Services

Inpatient Pharmacy: Medication management for hospitalized patients.

Outpatient Pharmacy: Prescription services for discharged patients and general public.
